Detailed Description

A solute reaction of 1st order kinetics (linear decline to product) A->B.

\[ A \rightarrow B \]

\[\frac{d[B]}{dt} = -\frac{d[A]}{dt} = k [A]\]

Where A is the educt and B is the product.
