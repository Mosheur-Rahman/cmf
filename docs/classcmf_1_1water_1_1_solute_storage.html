Detailed Description

A class for the storage of any tracer.

The state is the amount (mol, kg etc. see cmf::water) of the tracer in the storage.

The derivative function is given by:

\begin{eqnarray*} \frac{dX}{dt}&=&\sum_{f=1}^{F}\left( q_f [X]_f\right) + X_{in} - r^-X \left[\frac{mol}{day}\right]\\ F&=& \mbox{Number of fluxes in water storage} \\ q_f&=& \mbox{Water flux in } \frac{m^3}{day} \\ \left[X\right]_f &=& \mbox{Concentration of solute X in flux }q_f \mbox{ in } \frac{mol}{m^3} \\ X_{in} &=& \mbox{Absolute source or sink term} \frac{mol}{day} \\ r^- &=& \mbox{Decay rate} \frac 1{day} \\ V &=& \mbox{Volume of water in water storage }\left[m^3\right] \end{eqnarray*}
